About Us

Founded in September of 2013 and based in Tel Aviv, Israel, Famuza is an online marketplace that brings together the most talented fashion designers and the best manufacturers to create a collaborative designing platform - online or from a mobile phone.

Whether a top for work, dress for a night out, or everyday essentials, Famuza brings to people unique outfits, at fair price. And with world-class customer service and a growing community, Famuza is the best way for fashion designers to start a career in the fashion industry.
